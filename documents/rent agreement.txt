RENTAL AGREEMENT
THIS RENTAL AGREEMENT is made on this 15th day of May, 2025 at Mumbai
BETWEEN
Mr. Rajesh Kumar, Son of Mr. Suresh Kumar, aged about 48 years, residing at 123, Sea View Apartments, Bandra West, Mumbai - 400050, Maharashtra, India. (Hereinafter referred to as the "LANDLORD")
AND
Ms. Priya Sharma, Daughter of Mr. Anil Sharma, aged about 32 years, residing at 456, Green Valley, Powai, Mumbai - 400076, Maharashtra, India. (Hereinafter referred to as the "TENANT")
WHEREAS the Landlord is the absolute owner of the residential premises bearing Flat No. 302, 3rd Floor, Sunshine Apartments, Andheri East, Mumbai - 400069, Maharashtra, India, admeasuring about 850 sq. ft. carpet area (hereinafter referred to as the "PREMISES").
AND WHEREAS the Tenant has approached the Landlord to let out the said Premises to the Tenant for residential purpose and the Landlord has agreed to let out the same on the terms and conditions hereinafter appearing.
NOW THIS AGREEMENT WITNESSETH AND IT IS HEREBY AGREED BY AND BETWEEN THE PARTIES HERETO AS FOLLOWS:

TERM OF TENANCY:
The Landlord hereby lets out the Premises to the Tenant for a period of 11 (Eleven) months commencing from 1st June, 2025 and ending on 30th April, 2026.
RENT:
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord a monthly rent of Rs. 35,000/- (Rupees Thirty-Five Thousand Only) payable in advance on or before the 5th day of each English calendar month.
SECURITY DEPOSIT:
The Tenant has paid to the Landlord a sum of Rs. 1,05,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Five Thousand Only) being a refundable interest-free security deposit, the receipt of which the Landlord hereby acknowledges. The security deposit shall be refunded to the Tenant at the time of vacating the Premises after deducting unpaid rent, if any, and charges for damages caused to the Premises beyond normal wear and tear.
MAINTENANCE CHARGES:
The Tenant shall pay the monthly maintenance charges of Rs. 3,500/- (Rupees Three Thousand Five Hundred Only) directly to the Society.
ELECTRICITY AND WATER CHARGES:
The Tenant shall pay the electricity and water charges as per the actual consumption based on the respective meter readings.
USE OF PREMISES:
The Tenant shall use the Premises for residential purpose only and shall not use the same for any illegal or immoral purposes or for any purpose which may cause nuisance or annoyance to the neighbors or other occupants of the building.
REPAIRS AND MAINTENANCE:
a) The Landlord shall be responsible for major structural repairs.
b) The Tenant shall be responsible for minor day-to-day repairs and maintenance of the Premises.
c) The Tenant shall keep the Premises in good and tenantable condition.
LOCK-IN PERIOD:
There shall be a lock-in period of 6 (Six) months from the commencement of this Agreement. If the Tenant vacates the Premises before the expiry of the lock-in period, the Tenant shall be liable to pay the rent for the unexpired portion of the lock-in period.
RENEWAL:
This Agreement may be renewed for a further period as may be mutually agreed upon by both the parties. The intention to renew shall be communicated in writing at least 1 (One) month prior to the expiry of this Agreement.
TERMINATION:
After the lock-in period, either party may terminate this Agreement by giving 2 (Two) months' prior notice in writing to the other party.
RETURN OF POSSESSION:
Upon the expiration of the term of tenancy or earlier termination thereof, the Tenant shall peacefully and quietly vacate and hand over the possession of the Premises to the Landlord in the same condition as it was at the time of taking possession, subject to normal wear and tear.
GOVERNING LAW:
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
DISPUTE RESOLUTION:
Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved amicably between the parties. If such dispute cannot be resolved amicably within 30 days, it shall be referred to arbitration in accordance with the provisions of the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.
